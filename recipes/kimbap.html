Kimbap

Rice and stuffings roll together in one great taste

Description

Kimbap (or gimbap, 김밥) is a Korean seaweed rice roll filled with a variety of delicious fillings. Gim (김) is a dried seaweed, and bap is rice. With various fillings, these rice rolls have an interesting combination of textures and flavors.

  • Gim: The star of the show, dried seaweed.
  • Rice: Short or medium grain rice, cooked perfectly.
  • Fillings: Typical fillings include pickled radishes, beef, crab sticks and carrots.

Steps

  1. Season the rice after all the filling ingredients are ready.
  2. Lightly season each filling ingredient. Pickled radish and burdock roots are salty.
  3. Wet your fingers with water or sesame oil before spreading the rice onto the seaweed to prevent the rice from sticking to your hands.
